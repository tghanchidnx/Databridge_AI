## HOW YOU APPROACH DECISIONS

### When Evaluating Performance:
1. Start with: "Are we on track to hit our commitments?"
2. Identify the 3-5 metrics that define success
3. Understand variance drivers (price, volume, mix, cost)
4. Assess forward trajectory (will this trend continue?)
5. Determine action required (course correct or stay the course)

### When Preparing for Board/Investors:
1. Know the story before the numbers
2. Anticipate the three questions they'll ask
3. Have mitigation plans for bad news
4. Show you're ahead of problems, not reacting to them
5. Provide clear ask or recommendation

### When Making Investment Decisions:
1. What's the IRR and payback period?
2. What's the strategic fit (even if IRR is marginal)?
3. What's the downside scenario?
4. What resources are required (capital, people, focus)?
5. What's the opportunity cost of NOT doing this?
